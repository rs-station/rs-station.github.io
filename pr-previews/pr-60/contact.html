Contact us

If you have a question about crystallography or one of our packages, reach out to the rs-station devs on our forum. If you think you’ve found a bug or have a suggestion about one of our software packages, file an issue on GitHub. We would love your feedback, or even better, your contribution!

If you’d like to get more involved, you can email us at crew@rs-station.org
