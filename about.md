# What is Reciprocal Space Station?
Reciprocal Space Station (RSS) is a consortium developing open-source methods for the next generation of structural biology. Our tools span X-ray crystallography, cryo-EM, SAXS and the AI that increasingly connects structure to function. We're an open community that welcomes the involvement and contributions of academics, industry, and non-profits from across the world of structural biology.

RSS began with [`reciprocalspaceship`](https://github.com/rs-station/reciprocalspaceship), a crystallographically-aware extension of [`pandas`](https://github.com/pandas-dev/pandas) that was a first interface between the world of crystallography and scientific python & AI. Since then, RSS has grown into a broader collection of packages united by a common philosophy: open-source code, engineering and science as equals, and the belief that structural biology is about to blast beyond the single-structure paradigm.

We welcome contributors at all levels. If you build tools for structural biology and share these values, you too are a 1/🧑‍🚀!
